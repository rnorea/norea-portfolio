About Me

I am Software Development student from AUPP , a frontend developer and designer who loves turning complex ideas into clean, intuitive digital experiences. With a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I craft responsive, accessible interfaces that feel as good as they look. My design approach blends user-centered thinking with a sharp eye for visual detail, ensuring every product is both functional and engaging.
